1. Overview¶

These are my notes from the series of videos by Lars Brünjes for the Plutus Pioneers Program.

The source code for the lectures can be found here.

These notes can be downloaded in PDF or ePub formats using the links at the bottom left of the page. Both formats are auto-generated and probably look ok, but I’ve not yet spent any time making adjustments to work on any issues with their presentation.

Note

The Plutus Pioneer lectures were created at a time when Plutus was going through rapid development. As a result, earlier lectures rely on earlier versions of the Plutus code. Once Plutus has settled into a stable form, I will update all these notes to reflect this, and will make sure to highlight the differences between the video lectures and these written notes.
